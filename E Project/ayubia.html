A HIKE TO AYUBIA

Ayubia is a famous picnic spot in district Abbottabad, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, Pakistan. It is surrounded by the beautiful towns of Nathiagali, Thandiani and Khanspur. It has a distance of 40 kilometres from Abbottabad through the roads of Nathia Gali and Murree. There are many picnic spots, hiking places and riding trails in the region. The tourists love to see the forested mountains view through a chairlift in the centre of the region.The region contains mixed temperate forest and lies with an average elevation of 2400 meters (8000 feet) above the sea level.The regionâ€™s climate is cool in summer while the winter season is harsh and extremely cold in season. The summer season temperature varies from 3 to 4 degrees centigrade while the lowest recorded temperature in the winter season is -11 degrees centigrade. The average rainfall is 1244 cm. As Ayubia is one of the famous hiking spot of Pakistan, so here's begin our hiking trek.

HIKING TRACK

So, our hiking journey will start from Dunga gali. Enjoying the nature and the chirping of the birds will make this journey more beautiful. It takes 2 to 4 hours to hike. Don't forget to carry your back packs and hiking stick. A hike of 5km that will freshen your mind. A path that goes through lonh pine trees will absolutely make your day. Yeah! you are now in Pakistan. The beauty of mountains, hills and even forests will naturally shorten the time period. Here we will pass the Dunga gali or Pipeline Track. Now finally we will cross the borders of pipe line track and here we are in Ayubia.
